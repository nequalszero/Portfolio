About Me

Hi there! My name is Travis and I am a junior software engineer from San Jose, California. My Bachelor’s Degree is actually in Chemical Engineering, but while working as an intern for Visicon Technologies in Napa, I found my passion for coding. Since then, I have delved into the world of web development and software design and haven’t looked back! I hope to work as a full-stack developer with a fantastic team.

Outside of the tech world, I spend a lot of my time hammering away at the piano, making pizza and bubble tea, and training at the climbing gym for weekend adventures. I am always looking for great new climbing locations or backpacking excursions, and never miss the opportunity to see Valentina Lisitsa perform live!
